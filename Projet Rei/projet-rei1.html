Présentation


Our smart lamp provide the following functionnalities:
-Slow and confortable awakening imitating the natural way of waking up by copying the color and gleam change of dawn.
-Sound and light system are therefore designed to help people wake up more easily in the morning.
-Interesting design inspired by traditional japanese lamps.
